AI-Augmented Architectural Analysis

Why our methodology finds vulnerabilities automated scanners miss

01

Architecture Review

We use AI (Gemini, Claude) to analyze entire codebases for architectural patterns, not just syntax errors. This reveals business logic assumptions that become vulnerabilities.

02

Assumption Testing

Legacy systems assume perimeter security, session validation, and user honesty. We systematically challenge every assumption to find critical flaws.

03

Manual Verification

AI suggests potential vulnerabilities; we verify with manual testing, proof-of-concept development, and exploit chains. Only verified findings are reported.

04

Responsible Disclosure

We coordinate with MITRE for CVE assignments, notify vendors, and publish research only after appropriate timelines. Ethics first, always.
